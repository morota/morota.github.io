Join us

The Morota lab is located in the Department of Animal Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln on the East Campus. Our lab has close ties with other Animal Genetics faculty in the areas of molecular genetics, functional genomics, breeding and quantitative genetics.

Postdocs

We are always seeking vibrant and motivated individuals to join our lab. The ideal candidate will have a quantitative background and experience with analyzing large genomic data-sets. Strong programming skills and familiarity with major bioinformatics tools are essential. Informal inquiries are very welcome and should be sent to Gota Morota (morota@unl.edu).

Graduate students

Prospective graduate students who are interested in joining our team are welcome to email Gota Morota (morota@unl.edu) for available funding opportunities. The candidate will be trained in quantitative genetics, with a focus on applications to various types of omics data. Preference will be given to candidates with a strong interest in statistics. Good programming skills are highly desirable.
